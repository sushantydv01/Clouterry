WHY CREATOR ADS FAIL.

SLIDE 01

Brands often treat creators like actors in a commercial. This fundamental misunderstanding of audience psychology is why most campaigns burn budget without generating ROI.

STEP 1

Same brief.

Brands send the exact same brief to 20 different creators, ignoring their unique tone.

STEP 2

Forced script.

The creator is forced to read unnatural copy that doesn't sound like them.

STEP 3

Dead eyes.

The performance feels wooden. The audience can instantly tell it's a paid shill.

RESULT

Audience skips.
