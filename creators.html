FAQ

Do I need a huge audience?

Nope! If you make good stuff and people genuinely enjoy watching it, we want you in the club. Engagement matters way more than just a follower count.

Do I sign any contract?

No long-term commitments or weird management exclusivity clauses. You are completely independent. We just bring you opportunities.

How do opportunities work?

When a brand comes to us looking for your exact vibe, we reach out with the details, budget, and creative goals. If you like it, we move forward.

Do I have to accept campaigns?

Never. You always have 100% control over what you say yes to. If a brand doesn't fit your audience, just pass on it.

How do payments work?

We negotiate the best rate for you, secure the funds, and ensure you get paid promptly as soon as the campaign requirements are fulfilled. No chasing invoices.
